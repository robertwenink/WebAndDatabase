Draughts

A two player game

Goal

You have 12 game pieces.
Your goal is to immobilize all 12 pieces of your opponent, which either means all pieces have been captured or the remaining pieces cannot make a move anymore.

Rules

  • Moving is only done forward and diagonally by one tile over the dark tiles.

  • Capturing can be done by diagonally hopping over an oponent`s piece, however the space behind it must of course be vacant.

  • If one is able to capture, it is obliged. This counts for multiple captures with one piece as well.

  • If a piece reaches the last line of the opponent`s side, it is transformed in a King, and can now move and capture backwards as well!

  • If you are black or white is random. White begins.
